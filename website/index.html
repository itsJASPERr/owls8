who we are

OWLS8 is a community based on friendship and gaming as a common ground. We thrive to provide digital requirements for games and their players within the community for free. We always work together on challenges and achievements and try to get together in the non-digital world as often as possible.

The core of OWLS8, formerly known as arCadya-online.de, exists since 2007 and still contains the same people - the core has just expanded. We focus on trust in each other and a stable network as a community, we take our time to achieve great results. With confidence and integrity we work as a unit. Everyone has the right to speak and influence the development of the community.

If your philosophy sounds somewhat like ours and you want a chance to prove yourself as a part of OWLS8, feel free to contact us, via the contact form or a direct message to one of our founders, had-managers, or team managers. Everyone will be glad to hear from you and will try to get back to you ASAP.

what we play

We love games, we love gaming! It doesn't matter if casually or professionally. Community teams can be formed within the community and existing teams are welcome to join us, too.

Currently three teams with a constant lineup live under the wings of OWLS8 ;-). You can take a closer look right here, visit the team pages and player profiles, or even apply for a team. We are always looking forward to seeing new games being played by our community and new teams joining us. So if you want to build a team, or already have one - no matter which game - and want to play under the name OWLS8, contact us!
